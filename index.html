Daniel’s Growing Guides

Zone 6b

Important scope note:
These guides also do not cover soil amendment or preparation.

They are narrow, practical references for my own established home orchard/vineyard/garden.

They do NOT cover:
  • Variety selection
  • Rootstock selection or grafting
  • Initial tree structure / crotch-angle adjustment
  • General fruit-tree pruning (the grape guide only covers Concord renewal-zone management on Modified Munson)
  • Planting or long-term orchard establishment

They assume you already have mature or semi-mature trees/vines in place and are looking for specific renewal, spray scheduling, and seasonal maintenance details for Concord grapes and the mixed fruit plantings listed inside.

Please note: These guides are highly specific to the crops I grow in my area (Zone 6b), the exact disease and pest pressures I’m dealing with, and the chemicals I currently have on hand or plan to acquire. Spray rotations, product recommendations, and timing are tailored to my personal inventory and local conditions. They may not be fully applicable to other locations, different varieties, or different pest/disease profiles.

Available guides:

  • Fruit Spray & Management Guide (Grapes, Stone Fruit, Apple, Ribes, etc.)
  • Gardening Guide (mainly vegetables)
